Shooting Practice

Set up cones or markers to designate a shooting area. Receive a pass or dribble the ball into the shooting area. Practice shooting with different techniques: instep drive, chip shots, volleys, and bending shots. Aim for accuracy and consistency in hitting targets or specific areas of the goal.

Speed and Agility Ladder:

Lay an agility ladder on the ground. Stand at the ladder's beginning. Perform various ladder drills such as the "In and Out" (quickly stepping in and out of each box), the "Icky Shuffle" (side-stepping through the ladder), and "Quick Feet" (fast, alternate footwork). Focus on maintaining balance, speed, and coordination. Gradually increase the speed and complexity of the drills as you improve.

Free-Kick Practice:

Place the ball at the desired distance from the goal. Focus on your free-kick technique, including your approach, body posture, and foot placement. Aim for specific targets within the goal (e.g., corners) or practice bending the ball around a wall. Experiment with different techniques and learn to generate power and accuracy.
